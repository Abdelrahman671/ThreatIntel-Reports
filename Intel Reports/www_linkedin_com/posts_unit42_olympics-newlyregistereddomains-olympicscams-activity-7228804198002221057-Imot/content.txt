Use Trusted Channels: Only trust information from official sources and communication channels.

Report Suspicious Activity: If you receive any suspicious messages, report them to the authorities immediately.

        " dir="ltr" data-test-id="main-feed-activity-card__commentary">Safe Weekend Chaps 🍷

Martyn’s Law, 7 years in the making and still not quite over the line, but on the bright side, people are starting to prepare and it’s good to see blood kits being issued at venues, but can I suggest that everyone and we mean everyone download the CitizenAid app, which is free of change and simple to use, and covers just about every major traumatic event, from knife attack, active shooter, vehicle attack, explosions etc through to treatment, with a simple step by step guide even for those that are non medics. 

After every presentation I give, I add a slide about the App as I feel it can give confidence to people to aid someone that is injured. 

Having the bleed kits in venues is a great start, but let’s get everyone the confidence to use them even non medics, and this App is a great guide to how easy it is to save a life.
