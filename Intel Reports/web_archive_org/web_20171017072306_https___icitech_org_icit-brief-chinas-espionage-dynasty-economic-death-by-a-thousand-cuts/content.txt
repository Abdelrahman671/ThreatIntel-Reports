		<div id="attachment_5679" style="width: 242px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017072306/https://www.amazon.com/Chinas-Espionage-Dynasty-Economic-Thousand/dp/153532743X/ref=asap_bc?ie=UTF8" target="_blank"><img class="wp-image-5679 size-medium" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017072306im_/http://icitech.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/ChinasEspionageDynasty_V2-232x300.jpg" alt="ChinasEspionageDynasty_V2" width="232" height="300"/></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Download</p></div>
<p>The criminal culture of theft that has been injected into virtually every line of China’s 13th Five-Year Plan is unprecedented. From state sponsored smash and grab hacking and techno-pilfering, to corporate espionage and targeted theft of IP, never before in recorded history has IP transfer occurred at such a rapid velocity.</p>
<p>In this report, entitled &#8220;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017072306/https://www.amazon.com/Chinas-Espionage-Dynasty-Economic-Thousand/dp/153532743X/ref=asap_bc?ie=UTF8" target="_blank">China’s Espionage Dynasty: Economic Death by a Thousand Cuts</a>,&#8221; ICIT offers a comprehensive analysis of the primary structure of Chinese espionage initiatives and discuss the layers of espionage and theft as well as the malicious actors who carry out these overt and covert attacks on Western industry.  Specifically, this report discusses:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>China’s Thirteenth Five-Year Plan</strong></li>
<li><strong>Economic Impact of Intellectual Property Theft</strong></li>
<li><strong>The Structure of Chinese Espionage</strong>
<ul>
<li>Covert Spy Structure</li>
<li>Joint Staff Department</li>
<li>Second Department</li>
<li>Third Department</li>
<li>Fourth Department</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>State-Sponsored APTs</strong>
<ul>
<li>PLA Unit 61398/ APT 1/ Comment Panda/ Comment Crew/ TG-8223</li>
<li>PLA Unit 61486/ APT2/ Putter Panda/ TG-6952</li>
<li>Deep Panda/ APT 19/ Shell Crew/ Black Vine/ Kung Fu Kitten</li>
<li>PLA Unit 78020/ APT 30/ Naikon</li>
<li>Axiom/ Winnti Group</li>
<li>Hurricane Panda</li>
<li>Gothic Panda/ APT 3/ UPS/ Pirpi/ Clandestine Fox/ TG-0110</li>
<li>NetTraveler/ Travnet/ Netfile</li>
<li>Mirage/APT 15/ Vixen Panda/ Ke3Chang/ GREF/ Playful Dragon</li>
<li>Hellsing/ Goblin Panda</li>
<li>Stone Panda</li>
<li>Nightshade Panda</li>
<li>Anchor Panda</li>
<li>Numbered Panda/ APT 12/ IXESHE/ DYNCALC/ JOY RAT/ Etumbot</li>
<li>Hidden Lynx / Aurora</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Overt Spy Structure</strong>
<ul>

</ul>
</li>
</ul>
