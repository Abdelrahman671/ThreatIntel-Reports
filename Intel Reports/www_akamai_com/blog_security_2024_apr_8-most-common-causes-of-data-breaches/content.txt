<div data-cmp-is="text" class="cmp-text" data-cmp-data-layer="{&#34;text-14025cfbe8&#34;:{&#34;@type&#34;:&#34;akamai/components/text&#34;,&#34;repo:modifyDate&#34;:&#34;2024-04-17T03:01:43Z&#34;,&#34;xdm:text&#34;:&#34;&lt;p>Web application attacks contribute to&amp;nbsp;&lt;a href=\&#34;https://www.verizon.com/business/en-gb/resources/reports/dbir/\&#34; target=\&#34;_blank\&#34; rel=\&#34;nofollow noreferrer\&#34;>26% of breaches&lt;/a>, ranking as the second-most prevalent attack pattern.&lt;/p>\r\n&lt;p>On average, malicious bots represent over &lt;a href=\&#34;https://www.sitelock.com/resources/security-report/\&#34; target=\&#34;_blank\&#34; rel=\&#34;nofollow noreferrer\&#34;>60%&lt;/a> of all bot traffic on the internet.     <p>Web application attacks contribute to <a href="https://www.verizon.com/business/en-gb/resources/reports/dbir/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">26% of breaches</a>, ranking as the second-most prevalent attack pattern.</p>
<p>On average, malicious bots represent over <a href="https://www.sitelock.com/resources/security-report/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">60%</a> of all bot traffic on the internet. Moreover, <a href="https://www.ptsecurity.com/ww-en/analytics/web-vulnerabilities-2020-2021/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">17% of all cyberattacks</a> target vulnerabilities in web applications. <p>Application-layer attacks have spiked by as much as 80% in 2023, with 25,059 CVE vulnerabilities recorded in 2023: 5,000 more than 2022. Furthermore, 18% of websites are infected with critical severity threats such as backdoor and malicious file modifications.</p>
<p><b>Solution</b>: Ensure your <a href="/glossary/what-is-a-waf" target="_self">web application firewall</a> (WAF) is regularly updated and well-managed. It should also use an advanced artificial intelligence (AI) engine to close potential vulnerabilities and maintain a robust defense against unauthorized access.</p>
<p>The WAF should be a robust security solution designed to protect web applications from a variety of cyberthreats, including data breaches. It serves as a barrier between web applications and the internet, scrutinizing and filtering HTTP traffic to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities and attacks.</p>
<p>Key features of WAF protection include:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Advanced AI-based security policies </p>
</li>
<li><p>Advanced threat intelligence</p>
</li>
<li><p>Behavioral analysis </p>
</li>
<li><p>Scalability</p>
</li>
<li><p>Adaptive security</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Overall, a WAF is a comprehensive security solution that helps organizations secure their web applications, prevent data breaches, and ensure a robust defense against various cyberthreats.</p>

    <p>The number of unique <a href="/glossary/what-are-api-attacks" target="_self">API attacks</a> has increased by <a href="https://www.wallarm.com/resources/q2-2023-api-threatstats-tm-report#:~:text=The%20total%20number%20of%20unique,attacks%20have%20also%20escalated%20dramatically." target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">60%</a> from Q2 2022 to Q2 2023. APIs account for 83% of all <a href="https://apicontext.com/why-apis-are-eating-the-world-wide-web" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">web traffic</a>, making them a prime target for attackers. We also observed some interesting <a href="/resources/state-of-the-internet/lurking-in-the-shadows" target="_self">trends globally</a>, with the Europe, Middle East, and Africa (EMEA) region experiencing the greatest ratio of attacks that targeted APIs (47.5%), followed by North America (27.1%) and the Asia-Pacific and Japan (APJ) region (15%).     <p>Domain Name System (DNS) attacks are malicious activities that target the DNS infrastructure to disrupt or manipulate the resolution of domain names into IP addresses. These attacks can have various objectives, including causing service disruptions using distributed denial of service (DDoS), redirecting users to malicious websites, or gaining unauthorized access to sensitive information. </p>

<ul>
<li><p><a href="/glossary/what-is-dns-cache-poisoning" target="_self">DNS spoofing (cache poisoning)</a></p>

</li>
<li><p>DNS DDoS flood attack</p>
</li>
<li><p>DNS tunneling</p>
</li>
<li><p><a href="/blog/security/shield-ns53-protects-from-nxdomain-attacks" target="_self">NXDOMAIN attack</a></p>
</li>
<li><p><a href="/blog/trends/dnssec-how-it-works-key-considerations" target="_self">DNSSEC (DNS security extensions) attacks</a></p>
</li>
</ul>

<div class="text text__pb-20 aem-GridColumn aem-GridColumn--default--12">
<div data-cmp-is="text" class="cmp-text" data-cmp-data-layer="{&#34;text-eba3de6cba&#34;:{&#34;@type&#34;:&#34;akamai/components/text&#34;,&#34;repo:modifyDate&#34;:&#34;2024-04-17T03:37:50Z&#34;,&#34;xdm:text&#34;:&#34;&lt;p>Data breaches pose a pervasive risk across various sectors, affecting organizations of all sizes and types — from healthcare and finance to ecommerce and retail.&amp;nbsp;&lt;/p>\r\n&lt;p>By proactively identifying potential vulnerabilities, organizations can reduce the likelihood of successful cyberattacks.     <p>Data breaches pose a pervasive risk across various sectors, affecting organizations of all sizes and types — from healthcare and finance to ecommerce and retail. </p>
<p>By proactively identifying potential vulnerabilities, organizations can reduce the likelihood of successful cyberattacks. <p>By minimizing the impact of breaches, organizations can also maintain business continuity — and avoid disruptions to normal operations or damaged reputations. </p>
<p>Overall, a comprehensive understanding of breach causes and the implementation of appropriate security measures are vital for protecting data, minimizing risk, and ensuring the long-term success of all organizations.</p>
