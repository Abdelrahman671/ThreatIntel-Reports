			<p><em><strong>An insight into Transparent Tribe’s sub-division which has been incorrectly attributed for years.</strong></em></p>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Quick Heal’s threat intelligence team recently uncovered evidence of an advanced persistent threat (APT) against Indian defence forces. Our analysis shows that many old campaigns and attack in the past one year relate to &#8216;Operation SideCopy&#8217; by common IOCs.</p>
<h2><strong>Key Findings</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Operation SideCopy is active from early 2019, till date.</li>
<li>This cyber-operation has been only targeting Indian defence forces and armed forces personnel.</li>
<li>Malware modules seen are constantly under development and updated modules are released after a reconnaissance of victim data.</li>
<li>Actors are keeping track of malware detections and updating modules when detected by AV.</li>
<li>Almost all CnC belongs to Contabo GmbH and server names are similar to machine names found in the Transparent Tribe report.</li>
<li>This threat actor is misleading the security community by copying TTPs that point at Sidewinder APT group.</li>
<li>We suspect this threat actor has links with Transparent Tribe APT group.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Summary:</h2>
<p>A few months ago, Quick Heal’s Next-Gen Behavioural Detection system alerted on a few processes executing HTA from some non-reputed websites.</p>
<p>We have made a list of URLs, connected from mshta.exe, across multiple customers:</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-hospital[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/19/Armed-Forces-Spl-Allowance-Order/html/</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-hospital[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/19/Defence-Production-Policy-2020/html/</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-hospital[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/19/Images/8534</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-hospital[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/19/IncidentReport/html/</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-hospital[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/19/ParaMil-Forces-Spl-Allowance-Order/html/</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-hospital[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/19/Req-Data/html</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-hospital[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/19/Sheet_Roll/html</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-school[.]in/uploads/staff_documents/9/Sheet_Roll/html</p>
<p>hxxps://demo[.]smart-school[.]in/uploads/student_documents/12/css/</p>
<p>hxxps://drivetoshare[.]com/mod[.]gov[.]in_dod_sites_default_files_Revisedrates/html</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The highlighted ones were sent to targets across Indian defence units and armed forces individuals.</p>
<p>We started tracking this campaign as it was targeting critical Indian organizations.</p>
<p>Traces of this operation can be tracked from early 2019 till date. Till now, we have observed 3 infection chain process.</p>
<p>Initial infection vector in two of the chains was LNK file, that came from a malspam. But in one case, we saw attackers making use of template injection attack and equation editor vulnerability (CVE-2017-11882) as the initial infection vector. Though the initial infection vector is different in the third case, the final payload is similar to the first two chains.</p>
