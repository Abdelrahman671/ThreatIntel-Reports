        <h2 data-observer="waypoint-5-industry">Phase 2: Industrial espionage</h2>

        <p>By 2014, the Winnti malware code was no longer limited to game manufacturers. The second groupâs job is mainly industrial espionage.         <p>Most people probably know the DAX company Henkel as a manufacturer of detergents and shampoos.         <p>The Winnti hackers broke into Henkelâs network in 2014. We have three files showing that this happened.         <h2 data-observer="waypoint-10-politics">Political espionage?</h2>

        <p>Corporations like Bayer, Covestro, Roche and Bostik share a single common denominator: the chemical sector.
