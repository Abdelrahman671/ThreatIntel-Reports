		<h1>VB2021 localhost videos available on YouTube</h1>
		<blockquote class="pull-right">
		<small>Posted by &nbsp;<i class="glyphicon glyphicon-user"></i>&nbsp;<b></b> on &nbsp;<i class="glyphicon glyphicon-time"></i>&nbsp;<i>Nov 4, 2021</i></small>
		</blockquote>
		<div class="clearfix"></div>
		<p>Today, VB has made all VB2021 localhost presentations available on VB's <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/virusbtn/playlists" target="_blank"><em>YouTube</em> channel</a>, so you can now watch – and share – any part of the conference freely and without registration.</p>
<p><a href="https://vblocalhost.com/" target="_blank"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="/files/4616/1545/3745/VB2021-localhost.png" alt="VB2021-localhost.png" width="420" height="139" /></a></p>
<p> </p>
<p>The <a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLffioUnqXWkd26LcNhheDeFHHme6C4QIj" target="_blank">VB2021 / Live track</a> playlist contains the two live broadcasts presented by our host Angela Lamont, each of which features eight presentations and live Q&amp;A with the speakers.</p>
<p>Meanwhile, the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLffioUnqXWkeRQ4rTKzRxVdxJFxqngV7_" target="_blank">VB2021 / On-demand track</a> playlist features the 32 presentations that formed the VB2021 on-demand programme.</p>
<p>Finally, the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLffioUnqXWkeSkg-SX9mPnWQr4jxyp6ST" target="_blank">VB2021 / Threat Intelligence Practitioners' Summit</a> playlist features the presentations brought to you by our co-host the <em>Cyber Threat Alliance</em>.</p>
<p><span class="style-scope yt-formatted-string" dir="auto">The full programme, downloadable conference papers, presentation slides, speaker information and more can be found on <span class="style-scope yt-formatted-string" dir="auto">the <a href="https://vblocalhost.com/" target="_blank">VB2021 localhost website</a></span>.</span></p>		</div>
