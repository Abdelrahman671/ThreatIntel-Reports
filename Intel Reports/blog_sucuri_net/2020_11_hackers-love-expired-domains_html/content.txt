allow it to expire without attempting to renew it.</p><p>This happens all the time and is totally normal, but it’s important to remember that attackers regularly monitor domain expirations and may target certain domains that meet specific criteria.</p><h2>Vendor domains can be an easy backdoor</h2><p>A vendor (supplier) domain is defined as a website that is used to host and load third party
