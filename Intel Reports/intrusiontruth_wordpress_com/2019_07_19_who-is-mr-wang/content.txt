	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><span style="color:#000000;">In our last article we identified Jinan Quanxin Technology Co. Ltd. (<span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">济南全欣方沅科技有限公司</span></span>) and the Jinan Anchuang Information Technology Co. Ltd. (<span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">济南安创信息科技有限公司</span></span>) as companies associated with Guo Lin (<span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">郭林</span></span>), a likely MSS Officer in Jinan.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;"><b>Jinan Fanglang Information Technology Company</b></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">As disclosed previously by this blog, the antorsoft[.]com domain name listed the main address for Jinan Quanxin Fangyuan as 238, Jing Shi Dong Lu, Jinan, China.</span></p>
<p><span id="more-395"></span></p>
<figure data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-397" style="width: 487px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img data-attachment-id="397" data-permalink="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/2019/07/19/who-is-mr-wang/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped/" data-orig-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped.png" data-orig-size="487,177" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-meta="{&quot;aperture&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;credit&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;camera&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;caption&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;created_timestamp&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;copyright&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;focal_length&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;iso&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;shutter_speed&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;title&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;orientation&quot;:&quot;0&quot;}" data-image-title="5-antorsoft WHOIS cropped" data-image-description="" data-image-caption="" data-medium-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped.png?w=300" data-large-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped.png?w=487" class=" size-full wp-image-397 aligncenter" src="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped.png?w=700" alt="5-antorsoft WHOIS cropped"   srcset="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped.png 487w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped.png?w=150&amp;h=55 150w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/5-antorsoft-whois-cropped.png?w=300&amp;h=109 300w" sizes="(max-width: 487px) 100vw, 487px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-397" class="wp-caption-text">Historical WHOIS data for Antorsoft</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">But Jinan Quanxin Fangyuan wasn&#8217;t the only IT company registered at this address. We know that 238, Jing Shi Dong Lu, Jinan was also the registered address of the Jinan Fanglang Information Technology Co. Ltd. (<span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">济南方朗信息科技有限公司</span></span>). Let&#8217;s take a look at how we can prove it&#8230; </span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;"><b>iamjx aka phoenix</b></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Firstly, can we identify any staff who might work for Jinan Fanglang and tie them to hacking activity? The trail starts with a<a href="https://bbs.pediy.com/thread-197599.htm"> job that was advertised</a> for Jinan Fanglang on pediy[.]com by an individual using the handles iamjx and phoenix.</span></p>

<p><span style="color:#000000;">Although iamjx is a handle that is difficult to identify elsewhere in open source, our analysts were able to find a second job advert using the same QQ number, 8401900931. This second advert was on binvul[.]com. Unfortunately we can&#8217;t connect to binvul, but one of our analysts passed us a printout obtained from a location with better access.</span></p>

<p><span style="color:#000000;">The precise name of the company isn&#8217;t clear – it says <span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">济南安全公司招聘 </span></span>(Jinan Security Company Recruitment) – and the poster doesn&#8217;t give their name. But their link to the hacking world is obvious from another posting in 2012 using the same w4ngqw account on binvul, this time commenting on the impressive nature of CVE-2012-1848.</span></p>
<figure data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-402" style="width: 960px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img data-attachment-id="402" data-permalink="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/2019/07/19/who-is-mr-wang/8-cve-2012/" data-orig-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png" data-orig-size="960,570" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-meta="{&quot;aperture&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;credit&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;camera&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;caption&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;created_timestamp&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;copyright&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;focal_length&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;iso&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;shutter_speed&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;title&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;orientation&quot;:&quot;0&quot;}" data-image-title="8-cve-2012" data-image-description="" data-image-caption="" data-medium-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png?w=300" data-large-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png?w=700" loading="lazy" class=" size-full wp-image-402 aligncenter" src="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png?w=700" alt="8-cve-2012"   srcset="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png 960w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png?w=150&amp;h=89 150w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png?w=300&amp;h=178 300w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/8-cve-2012.png?w=768&amp;h=456 768w" sizes="(max-width: 960px) 100vw, 960px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-402" class="wp-caption-text">w4ngqw discussing CVE-2012-1848</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color:#000000;"><b>Wang Qingwei (<span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">王庆卫</span></span>)</b></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Analysing the handle w4ngqw, it seems clear that the family name is Wang. The given name uses the pinyin characters &#8216;qw&#8217;, restricting the number of candidate names in Chinese. Searching on these candidate names, analysts working with this blog have identified that the owner of w4ngqw is Jinan resident and Cyber security expert Wang Qingwei (<span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">王庆卫</span></span>).</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Company <a href="http://www.sd-n-tax.gov.cn/module/download/downfile.jsp?classid=0&amp;filename=160804090553232.xls" target="_blank" rel="noopener">tax registration information</a> obtained by this blog from the official website of Shandong Province lists the company <span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">济南方朗信息科技有限公司 </span></span>(Jinan Fanglang Information Technology Co. Ltd.) with a registration address of 238 Jing Shi Dong Lu, Jinan. Who was named as the company representative? <span style="font-family:Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span lang="zh-CN">王庆卫</span></span>&#8230;</span></p>
<figure data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-399" style="width: 1264px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img data-attachment-id="399" data-permalink="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/2019/07/19/who-is-mr-wang/9-tax-records/" data-orig-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png" data-orig-size="1264,452" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-meta="{&quot;aperture&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;credit&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;camera&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;caption&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;created_timestamp&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;copyright&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;focal_length&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;iso&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;shutter_speed&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;title&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;orientation&quot;:&quot;0&quot;}" data-image-title="9-tax records" data-image-description="" data-image-caption="" data-medium-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png?w=300" data-large-file="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png?w=700" loading="lazy" class=" size-full wp-image-399 alignnone" src="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png?w=700" alt="9-tax records"   srcset="https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png 1264w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png?w=150&amp;h=54 150w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png?w=300&amp;h=107 300w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png?w=768&amp;h=275 768w, https://intrusiontruth.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/9-tax-records.png?w=1024&amp;h=366 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1264px) 100vw, 1264px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-399" class="wp-caption-text">Tax registration information for Jinan Fanglang</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color:#000000;"><b>Let&#8217;s challenge the hypothesis</b></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">It could be argued that Wang Qingwei – the representative of a company that merely shared an office with a second company whose domain name was registered by Guo Lin – had nothing to do with Mr Guo or the MSS. How strange then that a source with access to such information informed us that Guo Lin and Wang Qingwei flew together on a multi-stop trip in 2016. But they weren&#8217;t just on the same plane, they sat next to each other on every leg of the flight&#8230;</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">We admit to not being data scientists here at Intrusion Truth, but if the population of China is 1.4 billion, the chances of sitting next to the same Chinese person on at least three journeys must be 1/(1,400,000,000)^3, which is to say 1 in 2,744,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Or 2.7 octillion to 1.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Which is quite low.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;"><b>In summary, Wang Qingwei, an IT security expert, advertised jobs at Jinan Fanglang using two online profiles and was also listed as the company&#8217;s official representative. He is directly linked to likely MSS Officer Guo Lin, travelling with him on multiple occasions.</b></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">#theyknowwherethisleads</span></p>
