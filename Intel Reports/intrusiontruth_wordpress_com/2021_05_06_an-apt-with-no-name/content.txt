<p class="has-normal-font-size" style="line-height:1.5;">When the 7<sup>th</sup> July indictment was released naming two Chinese hackers affiliated with the Guangdong State Security Department, it grabbed our interest. The indictment also mentions an unnamed MSS Officer 1. Who could this be? Let’s start with the named hackers… &nbsp;</p>
