<h4><strong>Conclusions</strong></h4>
The campaigns appear to primarily target government agencies and critical infrastructure in Southeast Asia. With particular focus on the following countries: Philippines, Vietnam, and Taiwan.<br />
From August 2nd onwards, the threat actor inserted a new module into its infection chain containing a 64-bit shellcode which then leads to the execution of a third stage with the Marte and Cobalt Strike beacons.<br />
The modus operandi of the cyber actor reflects the techniques of APTs of Chinese origin, it has been noted that the group is operational from Monday to Friday in hours compatible with Chinese ones.<br />
Although it was not possible to make a precise attribution, it could be a subgroup of <strong>APT41</strong>.<br />
