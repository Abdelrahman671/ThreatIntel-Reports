<h3 style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: verdana;"><b><span face="&quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif"> (шифровальщик-вымогатель, RaaS,&nbsp;</span></b>публикатор</span><b style="font-family: verdana;"><span face="&quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif">) <span style="color: #38761d;">(первоисточник)</span></span></b></h3><h3 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: verdana;"><b><span face="&quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif"><a href="https://translate.google.ru/translate?hl=ru&amp;tab=wT&amp;sl=ru&amp;tl=en&amp;u=https%3A%2F%2Fid-ransomware.blogspot.com%2F2020%2F09%2Fegregor-ransomware.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Translation into English</a></span></b></span></h3>
