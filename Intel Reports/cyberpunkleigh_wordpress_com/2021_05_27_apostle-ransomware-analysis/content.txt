<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What is this Ransomware?</h2>



<p>Apostle ransomware appears to be a ransomware connected with attacks on israel with IOC&#8217;s many reports pointing towards Iran APTs but also a group formed in 2020 dubbed &#8220;<strong>Agrius</strong>&#8220;.</p>



<p>This ransomware is another one developed in .NET which as seen recently is starting to become a trend which is very good for us not so good for the bad guy.</p>
