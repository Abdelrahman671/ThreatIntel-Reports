</h3>

<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5350860383234313686' itemprop='description articleBody'>
One more targeted attack against Ukraine that used spear phishing to deliver the DarkTrack backdoor through a fake prescription of the Minister of Defense of Ukraine. The target is CERT in the military domain.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhrgSnfsu21BWhZQd4v2jNIFaI2a3F35f6EukmXwVJ9ahd2Ir1CuCGhtRdIjHaMm7WO1r36ve7gWd2xNSoQ0CULMsjpkI_nyUnlMixSzzlNiwn_yO4_IhhMCNdT4SUp4yYfX4HTHXVRuaRH/s1600/mail.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="288" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhrgSnfsu21BWhZQd4v2jNIFaI2a3F35f6EukmXwVJ9ahd2Ir1CuCGhtRdIjHaMm7WO1r36ve7gWd2xNSoQ0CULMsjpkI_nyUnlMixSzzlNiwn_yO4_IhhMCNdT4SUp4yYfX4HTHXVRuaRH/s640/mail.png" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<a name='more'></a><br />
<br />
The letter forces a receiver to download the prescription by the link until April 13, 2017.<br />
<br />
The domain 'fex.net' in the link has been actively used to distribute malware:<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiUDqX_FAKeMznG7tOq4gSSCzDPEbNvxgi2AWUPwrWNLet5M3K-CXJweo4hIxQd_EWWgFH7lwW3l-aI36TkkCUEqLnVYPiWTfDo3S4GAHTjNjeFza9BBN3oC1hdVxwhPZJYCWT1ZYsbkTQ9/s1600/fexnet.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="297" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiUDqX_FAKeMznG7tOq4gSSCzDPEbNvxgi2AWUPwrWNLet5M3K-CXJweo4hIxQd_EWWgFH7lwW3l-aI36TkkCUEqLnVYPiWTfDo3S4GAHTjNjeFza9BBN3oC1hdVxwhPZJYCWT1ZYsbkTQ9/s640/fexnet.png" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
The downloaded&nbsp;file 'розпорядження Полторак.docx.exe' is an obfuscated .NET application (MD5: <a href="https://www.virustotal.com/ru/file/e9c9c84aa83e908c0e978c5c8f9a03c665fc559ee6b9d511c911ac9abc61326c/analysis/">01fb11b245a6a2525da77aebd2879dcf</a>). It copies itself as:<br />
<ul>
<li>c:\Documents and Settings\&lt;USER&gt;\Templates\winlogon.exe</li>
</ul>
<div>
And drops the clean Word document:</div>
<ul>
<li>c:\Documents and Settings\&lt;USER&gt;\Local Settings\Temp\Docum.doc (MD5: b77f006667dd0a68de9c8ea30f2c80fe)</li>
</ul>
First, it executes 'C:\WINDOWS\system32\svchost.exe' and injects the Darktrack in the 'svchost.exe' process.<br />
<br />
Then, it opens clean 'Docum.doc' to take a user's attention away.<br />
The following message is shown on execution:<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiUsniq2Bt4ZDYYucGrkVwL0TOxqFBXPhDgaVVDYsY0Kdy2cXYaZOQMS8X6FNFR8BgYj8YSRFdygJs4yZrBbYFm5YbYFjnIZTrf2vEic3y8CARiiCR7sZngV6mIBx-jsekhd4-V4hHEdhCv/s1600/word97.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="103" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiUsniq2Bt4ZDYYucGrkVwL0TOxqFBXPhDgaVVDYsY0Kdy2cXYaZOQMS8X6FNFR8BgYj8YSRFdygJs4yZrBbYFm5YbYFjnIZTrf2vEic3y8CARiiCR7sZngV6mIBx-jsekhd4-V4hHEdhCv/s400/word97.png" width="400" /></a></div>
<br />
Then, it opens the embedded document:<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiwutDi98PXGatlBx5eeUE4Mi7ljuYNFyX7jU-uxSsL7FXoSdObf73yEGv3b5kEDlzWcpSZZNFA7peMvdcRVtm0zLiCKzrQ2CU64iBAO4MnwdRoPoqdFFXFqoxAHlSwu7NemiE4nQFUDWbg/s1600/document.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="595" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiwutDi98PXGatlBx5eeUE4Mi7ljuYNFyX7jU-uxSsL7FXoSdObf73yEGv3b5kEDlzWcpSZZNFA7peMvdcRVtm0zLiCKzrQ2CU64iBAO4MnwdRoPoqdFFXFqoxAHlSwu7NemiE4nQFUDWbg/s640/document.png" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">

<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">

<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhjRIYMRy7Hj8I3dMnoq1yvaWU5H_4QsjFgECjO-Al2ZYavD01z0RYH1zxAFVlb7sLFEMltNNrkYdLRzONSDdKsgMI5nYa65l7fECOVpA13pOA11lMaWH1gHlrY_Xa-4o4KxWnktGIfj6AB/s1600/image_dump.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="400" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhjRIYMRy7Hj8I3dMnoq1yvaWU5H_4QsjFgECjO-Al2ZYavD01z0RYH1zxAFVlb7sLFEMltNNrkYdLRzONSDdKsgMI5nYa65l7fECOVpA13pOA11lMaWH1gHlrY_Xa-4o4KxWnktGIfj6AB/s400/image_dump.png" width="318" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiKsZ0zFPV_PEelweSuDpSL1u7kdh7vxsmwbof9ZiaWeYuXnKes9B752mvcVOH6Pja5KvFzTa9QZhJybiTrSQpV46msRtoZEQMKHrNydZK0_CRhqLe3qQVt1SzFitII7rpFUC-BuZ0SwC61/s1600/inject.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="428" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiKsZ0zFPV_PEelweSuDpSL1u7kdh7vxsmwbof9ZiaWeYuXnKes9B752mvcVOH6Pja5KvFzTa9QZhJybiTrSQpV46msRtoZEQMKHrNydZK0_CRhqLe3qQVt1SzFitII7rpFUC-BuZ0SwC61/s640/inject.png" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
The backdoor is the&nbsp;Darktrack&nbsp;remote administration tool.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEilnyHZFZ0lNmaKEq0RAkNEMeY62bIusMi9_yq4tPHSmDq1MztX6zNaQpxz2-MajeU6FHDI2u7pXV08L4C8uT0cpDq5k2wa-ZfjGM92WFUtdiMQ41wx3YzJ7eXV5oQAzmOXmy94Cy2PXnRS/s1600/darktrack.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="488" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEilnyHZFZ0lNmaKEq0RAkNEMeY62bIusMi9_yq4tPHSmDq1MztX6zNaQpxz2-MajeU6FHDI2u7pXV08L4C8uT0cpDq5k2wa-ZfjGM92WFUtdiMQ41wx3YzJ7eXV5oQAzmOXmy94Cy2PXnRS/s640/darktrack.png" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
The client connects to the C&amp;C's 1515 port.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEioBuNDNKA1VI9639WEdrFCUiDVzsvypfOvDOe1c64M-VVT_7nr9FOZ75JOlGWt59Tg2kWlkqgRtmfjECqdDw1UeluiZNoN1URBn0442YnlU6c4wEOrQAXsZiGwJL-JTELka76aUm3XWemr/s1600/connection.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="400" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEioBuNDNKA1VI9639WEdrFCUiDVzsvypfOvDOe1c64M-VVT_7nr9FOZ75JOlGWt59Tg2kWlkqgRtmfjECqdDw1UeluiZNoN1URBn0442YnlU6c4wEOrQAXsZiGwJL-JTELka76aUm3XWemr/s400/connection.png" width="317" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
