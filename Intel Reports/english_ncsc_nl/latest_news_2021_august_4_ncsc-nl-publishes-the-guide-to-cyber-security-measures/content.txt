  <h1 class="news">
      NCSC-NL publishes the Guide to Cyber Security Measures</h1>
<p class="article-meta">
  News item | 05-08-2021 | 13:35</p><div class="intro"><p>To increase the digital resilience of companies and organisations, the NCSC-NL has published the newly translated <a href="/publications/publications/2021/august/4/guide-to-cyber-security-measures">Guide to Cyber Security Measures</a>. This lists eight measures that every organisation should take to prevent cyber-attacks.</p></div>
  <p>Examples of these measures are enabling logging, implementing multi-factor authentication, creating backups and encrypting sensitive information. Recent digital incidents show that companies and organisations are vulnerable if these measures are not taken.</p><div class="block docs-pubs results">
