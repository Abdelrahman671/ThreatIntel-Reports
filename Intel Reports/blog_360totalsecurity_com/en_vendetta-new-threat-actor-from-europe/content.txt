								<p>Starting in April this year, 360 Baize Lab intercepted a large number of attack samples from an unknown hacker organization. The hacker organization sent a phishing email to the victim by forging a police station investigation letter, COVID-19 detection notice, etc. , Through the backdoor virus to control the victim&#8217;s machine, steal valuable sensitive data related to the target.</p>
<p>The PDB path of the virus samples used by the organization points to a user named &#8220;Vendetta&#8221;, and we will later also name the hacker organization Vendetta:</p>
<p>“C:\Users\<strong>Vendetta</strong>\source\repos\{project name}\*\obj\Debug\{project name}.pdb”</p>
<p>In some samples, we have repeatedly detected the following tags, the virus author claims that he is from Italy:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.1.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25108" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.1.png" alt="" width="355" height="55" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.1.png 355w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.1-300x46.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 355px) 100vw, 355px" /></a></p>
<p>However, we found in the naming of virus samples that virus authors like to use certain Turkish names to name variables, such as &#8220;RoboSki&#8221;, so we suspect that the organization originated in Europe:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.2.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25112" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.2.png" alt="" width="658" height="275" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.2.png 658w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.2-300x125.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 658px) 100vw, 658px" /></a></p>
<p>Vendetta is a hacker organization that is very good at using social engineering. They forge phishing emails very realistically. They can easily gain users&#8217; trust and guide users to open the malicious programs they carry.</p>
<p>The picture below is a Vendetta forgery of the investigation letter issued by the Austrian Federal Ministry of the Interior (BMI)</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.3.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25116" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.3.png" alt="" width="755" height="1080" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.3.png 755w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.3-210x300.png 210w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.3-716x1024.png 716w" sizes="(max-width: 755px) 100vw, 755px" /></a></p>
<p>Forged investigation letter from the Romanian police station:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.4.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25120" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.4.png" alt="" width="1236" height="812" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.4.png 1236w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.4-300x197.png 300w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.4-768x505.png 768w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.4-1024x673.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1236px) 100vw, 1236px" /></a></p>
<p>Forged the COVID-19 virus test email issued by the Australian Government Department of Health. The email stated that the victim had contact with a confirmed case within the past 14 days. It is recommended to read the test guide in the attachment and accept the test:<a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.5.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25124" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.5.png" alt="" width="1010" height="1379" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.5.png 1010w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.5-220x300.png 220w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.5-768x1049.png 768w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.5-750x1024.png 750w" sizes="(max-width: 1010px) 100vw, 1010px" /></a></p>
<p>Forged a virus test email issued by the Mexican health department:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.6.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25128" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.6.png" alt="" width="1010" height="1100" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.6.png 1010w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.6-275x300.png 275w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.6-768x836.png 768w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.6-940x1024.png 940w" sizes="(max-width: 1010px) 100vw, 1010px" /></a></p>

<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.7.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25132" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.7.png" alt="" width="1010" height="1154" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.7.png 1010w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.7-263x300.png 263w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.7-768x877.png 768w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.7-896x1024.png 896w" sizes="(max-width: 1010px) 100vw, 1010px" /></a></p>
<p>The compressed file in the email attachment contains the Trojan file, which is generally named after pdf.exe, Document.exe, etc. After running, it decrypts and loads the subsequent virus module in memory.</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.8.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25136" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.8.png" alt="" width="764" height="343" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.8.png 764w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.8-300x135.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 764px) 100vw, 764px" /></a></p>
<h2>RoboSki</h2>
<p>In all samples, we detected the same type of code obfuscator, and according to its PDB debugging path, we named it RoboSki:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.9.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25140" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.9.png" alt="" width="604" height="52" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.9.png 604w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.9-300x26.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 604px) 100vw, 604px" /></a></p>
<p>RoboSki encrypts and stores the shellcode in the pixels of the picture. The following figure is the code logic to extract the available pixel data and decrypt the shellcode:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.10.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25144" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.10.png" alt="" width="508" height="646" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.10.png 508w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.10-236x300.png 236w" sizes="(max-width: 508px) 100vw, 508px" /></a></p>
<p><strong>ReZer0</strong></p>
<p>The execution logic of ReZer0 is controlled by hard-coded built-in instructions. According to different instructions, different malicious functions are executed. Its design logic resembles the design method of backdoor programs:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.11.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25148" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.11.png" alt="" width="698" height="717" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.11.png 698w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.11-292x300.png 292w" sizes="(max-width: 698px) 100vw, 698px" /></a></p>

<p>In the 360 massive data, we found that ReZer0 has an obvious version identification. In conjunction with the above-mentioned large number of instructions used, we speculate that the software is still in the development stage, and it will not be ruled out that the program will be controlled through network communication in the future:</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.12.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25152" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.12.png" alt="" width="845" height="648" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.12.png 845w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.12-300x230.png 300w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.12-768x589.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 845px) 100vw, 845px" /></a></p>
<p>In addition to the nature of the backdoor virus, ReZer0 also carries known remote control Trojans such as NanoCore and Remcos in the resources. We will not repeat the remote control functions such as NanoCore. We take some of the victims of Vendetta as an example to speculate the purpose of their actions.</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.13.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25156" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.13.png" alt="" width="1010" height="528" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.13.png 1010w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.13-300x157.png 300w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.13-768x401.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1010px) 100vw, 1010px" /></a></p>
<p>Passion Fruit Company of Australia (PAI) is a representative institution and a non-profit membership organization that supports the passion fruit industry in Australia. PAI is an umbrella organization that represents and enhances the interests of everyone in the passion fruit industry, including growers, packers, wholesalers, exporters, researchers, and retail stores.</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.14.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25160" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.14.png" alt="" width="1503" height="1016" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.14.png 1503w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.14-300x203.png 300w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.14-768x519.png 768w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.14-1024x692.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1503px) 100vw, 1503px" /></a></p>
<p>Of course, Vendetta&#8217;s attack target is not only the PAI family. We have roughly described the distribution of Vendetta&#8217;s attack target by statistically the distribution of related samples, and its attack purpose is to steal related commercial information.</p>
<p><a href="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.15.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25164" src="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.15.png" alt="" width="1404" height="715" srcset="https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.15.png 1404w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.15-300x153.png 300w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.15-768x391.png 768w, https://static.ts.360.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/514.15-1024x521.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1404px) 100vw, 1404px" /></a></p>

<p>Vendetta is an active hacking organization that started in April 2020. The organization may have originated in Europe. It is good at using social engineering to launch cyber attacks. The purpose of the attack is to steal targeted business intelligence.</p>
