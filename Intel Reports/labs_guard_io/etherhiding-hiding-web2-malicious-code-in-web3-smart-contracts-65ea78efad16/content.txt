Due to the publicly accessible and unchangeable nature of the blockchain, code can be hosted “on-chain” without the ability for a takedown.</p><p id="35a2" class="pw-post-body-paragraph mh mi gu mj b mk ml mm mn mo mp mq mr ms mt mu mv mw mx my mz na nb nc nd ne gn bk"><strong class="mj gv">This is what we see here in this attack — malicious code is hosted and served in a manner that can’t be blocked. </strong>Unlike hosting it on a Cloudflare Worker service as was mitigated on the earlier variant.
