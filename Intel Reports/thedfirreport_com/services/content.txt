<h1 class="entry-title">Services</h1> </header>
<div class="entry-content">
<figure class="wp-block-image size-medium"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/threat-intelligence/"><img decoding="async" width="300" height="75" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/threatintel-2-300x75.jpg" alt class="wp-image-21304" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/threatintel-2-300x75.jpg 300w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/threatintel-2-1024x256.jpg 1024w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/threatintel-2-768x192.jpg 768w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/threatintel-2-1536x384.jpg 1536w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/threatintel-2-2048x512.jpg 2048w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></figure>
<p></p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/threat-intelligence/">Threat Intelligence</a></h3>
<p>Gain access to a comprehensive suite of threat intelligence, encompassing everything from raw data from our public reports, to specialized threat feeds, to in-depth tracking of adversary infrastructure, exclusive private reports, and more.</p>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-medium"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/detection-rules/"><img decoding="async" width="300" height="75" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/detection-2-300x75.jpg" alt class="wp-image-21305" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/detection-2-300x75.jpg 300w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/detection-2-1024x256.jpg 1024w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/detection-2-768x192.jpg 768w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/detection-2-1536x384.jpg 1536w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/detection-2-2048x512.jpg 2048w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></figure>
<p></p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/detection-rules/">Detection Rules</a></h3>
<p>Our Private Ruleset is curated using insights derived from&nbsp;<a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/threat-intelligence/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Private Threat Briefs</a>&nbsp;and internal cases, focusing on Sigma rules. As of January 2024, it encompasses approximately 100 Sigma rules, created from the knowledge of 40+ distinct cases. Each rule is mapped to ATT&amp;CK and accompanied by a test example.</p>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-medium"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/dfir-labs/"><img decoding="async" width="300" height="75" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/labsfeat-1-300x75.jpeg" alt class="wp-image-31052" style="object-fit:cover" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/labsfeat-1-300x75.jpeg 300w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/labsfeat-1-1024x256.jpeg 1024w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/labsfeat-1-768x192.jpeg 768w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/labsfeat-1-1536x384.jpeg 1536w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/labsfeat-1-2048x512.jpeg 2048w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></figure>
<p></p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/dfir-labs/">DFIR Labs</a></h3>
<p>Our cloud-based DFIR (Digital Forensics and Incident Response) Labs offer a hands-on learning experience, using <strong>real</strong> data from <strong>real</strong> intrusions. Experience the world of digital forensics in a practical setting.</p>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-medium"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/case-artifacts/"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="300" height="75" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/artifacts1-300x75.jpg" alt class="wp-image-21308" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/artifacts1-300x75.jpg 300w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/artifacts1-1024x256.jpg 1024w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/artifacts1-768x192.jpg 768w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/artifacts1-1536x384.jpg 1536w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/artifacts1-2048x512.jpg 2048w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></figure>
<p></p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/case-artifacts/">Case Artifacts</a></h3>
<p>This service includes case artifacts from public reports including IOCs. These artifacts may include Event logs, Zeek logs, memory and packet captures, ransomware files, and other intrusion related files such C2 binaries. This service will also grant you access to our Threat Intel Platform.</p>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-medium is-style-default"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/mentoring-coaching-program/"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="300" height="75" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/services4-300x75.png" alt class="wp-image-21306" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/services4-300x75.png 300w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/services4-1024x256.png 1024w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/services4-768x192.png 768w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/services4-1536x385.png 1536w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/services4-2048x513.png 2048w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></figure>
<p></p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/services/mentoring-coaching-program/">Mentoring &amp; Coaching Program</a></h3>
<p>Designed for both aspiring and seasoned professionals in information security, we aim to elevate your skills and career prospects. Powered by the experts behind “The DFIR Report,” we bring you focused, personalized sessions in both mentoring and coaching.</p>
