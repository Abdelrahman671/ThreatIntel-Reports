		addEvent(evts[i], logHuman);

			<h1 class="entry-title">Defender Control</h1>
		<div class="entry-meta">
			<span class="posted-on"><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/2020/12/13/defender-control/" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date published" datetime="2020-12-13T22:00:09+00:00">December 13, 2020</time></a></span>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

		
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><a href="https://www.sordum.org/9480/defender-control-v1-6/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Defender Control</a> is a free software utility we&#8217;ve come across in various intrusions. The creators describe it by saying the following:</p>
<blockquote><p>What is certain however is that it [Windows Defender] will annoy users who want to disable it permanently on the computer they are working on. Defender Control is a small Portable freeware which will allow you to disable Windows Defender in Windows 10 completely.</p></blockquote>
<p>While we have not seen this in many of our recent intrusions with big game ransomware, it is common among the smaller players. Those like Dharma, Phobos, and Crysis. In our experience, these groups&#8217; main point of entry tends to be exposed RDP. After gaining access, these threat actors often do not take the time or effort to fully scope, or compromise a domain before ransoming, usually a single system. We&#8217;ve seen these threat actors get blocked by Defender and then minutes later Defender Control gets copied over and run.</p>
<p>Defender Control is two files, which are a portable executable and a configuration file.</p>
<p><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-5.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1525" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-5.png" alt="" width="272" height="81" /></a></p>
<p>Defender Control can be run via GUI or command line</p>
<p><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1602" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/defendercmd.png" alt="" width="364" height="331" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/defendercmd.png 364w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/defendercmd-300x273.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 364px) 100vw, 364px" /></p>
<p>This is an up to date Windows 10 system, with all Windows Defender controls active.</p>
<p><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-1.png"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-1520 size-full" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-1.png" alt="" width="1057" height="803" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-1.png 1057w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-1-300x228.png 300w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-1-1024x778.png 1024w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-1-768x583.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1057px) 100vw, 1057px" /></a></p>
<p>Let&#8217;s click Disable Windows Defender.</p>
<p><a href="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-2.png"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-1521 size-full" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-2.png" alt="" width="1058" height="806" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-2.png 1058w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-2-300x229.png 300w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-2-1024x780.png 1024w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/control-2-768x585.png 768w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 1058px) 100vw, 1058px" /></a></p>
<p>We can now see that the entire menu has vanished, and Windows tells you that the Organization is managing Defender.</p>
<h3>MITRE ATT&amp;CK</h3>
<h4>Defense Evasion &#8211; T1562.001</h4>
<p>Defender Control sets a couple registry values to disable Defender including this one:</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-1600" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/antispy.png" alt="" width="576" height="162" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/antispy.png 466w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/antispy-300x84.png 300w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 576px) 100vw, 576px" /></p>
<pre>HKLM\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Windows Defender\DisableAntiSpyware DWORD (0x00000001)</pre>
<p>Defender Control also sets this value to disable the startup of the Defender service:</p>
<pre>HKLM\System\CurrentControlSet\Services\WinDefend\Start DWORD (0x00000003)</pre>
<p>Which creates:</p>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1607" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/70402.png" alt="" width="634" height="274" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/70402.png 634w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/70402-300x130.png 300w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 634px) 100vw, 634px" /> <img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1608" src="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/7040.png" alt="" width="666" height="275" srcset="https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/7040.png 666w, https://thedfirreport.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/7040-300x124.png 300w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 666px) 100vw, 666px" /></p>
<p>Defender Control also unloads the Defender driver:</p>
