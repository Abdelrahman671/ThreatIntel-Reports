<div class="mod mod-contentpage">
    <h1 class="text-inline">Information on GovCERT</h1>
</div>

</div>	






<div class="mod mod-text">
    
        
        
            <article class="clearfix">
                
                
                    <p class="lead">The Government Computer Emergency Response Team (GovCERT) is the national specialist service responsible for the technical management of cyberincidents and technical analysis of cyberthreats. It supports critical infrastructure operators, the public sector and the Swiss business location with technical information on current cyberthreats and with the management of cyberincidents. GovCERT also works in close collaboration with the police authorities. This cooperation covers both the exchange of information and support with technical analyses.</p>
                
            </article>
        
    
</div>
<div class="mod mod-text">
    
        
        
            <article class="clearfix">
                
                    <p>To ensure the timely exchange of information on cyberthreats, GovCERT works with other CERTs and CSIRTs (computer security incident response teams) worldwide. In addition, GovCERT is a member of various recognised international organisations.</p>

                
                
            </article>
        
    
</div>
<div class="mod mod-anchorlist">
	
		
	  	
	  		
			
				<ul>
					 
					 	<li class="anchor"><a href="#-457446203" class="icon icon--before icon--root">Blog and white papers</a></li>

					 	<li class="anchor"><a href="#-1793729127" class="icon icon--before icon--root">GovCERT on Twitter/X</a></li>
					 
				</ul>
			
		
	
</div>

<div class="mod mod-headline" id="-457446203">
	
		
		
		
			<h2>Blog and white papers</h2>
		
	
</div>

<div class="mod mod-text">
    
        
        
            <article class="clearfix">
                
                    <p>Over the years, GovCERT has published a variety of blogs, white papers (technical reports) and other documentation on cyberthreats (previously on www.govcert.ch).<br>
These documents will now be published on GitHub:</p>

                
                
            </article>
        
    
</div>
<div class="mod mod-link">
	
		
		
			<p>
				<a href="https://github.com/govcert-ch/website/" aria-label="GitHub: Archive of the GovCERT.ch website" target="_blank" class="icon icon--after icon--external" title="">GitHub: Archive of the GovCERT.ch website</a>
			</p>
		
	
</div>
<div class="mod mod-headline" id="1737483390">
	
		
		
		
			<h2>List of blocked file types</h2>
		
	
</div>

<div class="mod mod-text">
    
        
        
            <article class="clearfix">
                
                    <p>GovCERT publishes a list of file types that are often used to distribute malware by email, and recommends that these be proactively blocked at the email gateway or spam filter, or placed in quarantine.</p>

                
                
            </article>
        
    
</div>

	</p>
	
	
</div>
<div class="mod mod-headline" id="-1476937530">
	
		
		
		
			<h2>International cooperation</h2>
		
	
</div>

<div class="mod mod-text">
    
        
        
            <article class="clearfix">
                
                    <p>In the joint fight against cyberthreats, international cooperation is very important. Membership in various associations enables GovCERT to establish a global network of personal relationships. GovCERT is a member of FIRST (Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams, www.first.org), of the European Government CERTs (EGC) group (www.egc-group.org) and of the International Watch and Warning Network (IWWN). In addition, GovCERT has accreditation from the European&nbsp;Trusted Introducer (TI) Service.</p>

                
                
            </article>
        
    
</div>
<div class="mod mod-link">
	
		
		
			<p>
				<a href="https://www.first.org/members/teams/ncsc-ch" aria-label="Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams FIRST" target="_blank" class="icon icon--after icon--external" title="">Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams FIRST</a>
			</p>
		
	
</div>
<div class="mod mod-link">

</div>
<div class="mod mod-link">

                    <h3><b>Reports on cyberincidents from critical infrastructures</b></h3>
<p>Reports on cyberincidents from critical infrastructures or cybersecurity specialists can be sent to GovCERT at the following email address:</p>
<p>incidents[at]govcert{dot}ch</p>
<h3><b>Technical queries</b></h3>
<p>Other technical queries that do not concern a specific cyberincident can be sent to GovCERT at the following email address:</p>
<p>outreach[at]govcert{dot}ch</p>
