class="blog-post-wrapper"><div itemprop="articleBody" class="rich-text-block w-richtext"><h2>Executive Summary</h2><p>Menlo Labs recently identified a phishing campaign targeting executives in senior level roles across various industries, but primarily Banking and Financial services, Insurance providers, Property Management and Real Estate, and Manufacturing.</p><p>The key findings based on our research of the phishing campaign are as follows:</p><ul role="list"><li>The campaign started in July and has continued into the month of August.</li><li>The campaign used a sophisticated phishing kit search platform âindeed.comâ, redirecting victims to malicious phishing pages impersonating Microsoft.</li></ul><p>This is a classic example of AiTM (Adversary In The Middle) phishing attack by
