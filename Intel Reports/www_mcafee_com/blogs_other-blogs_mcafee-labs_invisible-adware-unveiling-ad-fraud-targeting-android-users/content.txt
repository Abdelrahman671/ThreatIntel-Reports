<h2><span class="TextRun SCXW88765830 BCX0" lang="EN-US" xml:lang="EN-US" data-contrast="none"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW88765830 BCX0" data-ccp-parastyle="heading 2">How does it work?</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW88765830 BCX0" data-ccp-props="{&quot;134245418&quot;:true,&quot;134245529&quot;:true,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335559738&quot;:40,&quot;335559739&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:259}"> </span></h2>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0" lang="EN-US" xml:lang="EN-US" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">This ad fraud library </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">uses</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0"> specific tactics to avoid detection and inspection. </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">I</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">t </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">deliberately </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">delays the initiation of its fraudulent activities, creating a latent period</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0"> from the time of installation</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">. </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">What&#8217;s</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0"> more, all the intricate configurations of this library can be remotely </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">modified</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0"> and </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">pushed </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW155932530 BCX0">using Firebase Storage or Messaging service.
