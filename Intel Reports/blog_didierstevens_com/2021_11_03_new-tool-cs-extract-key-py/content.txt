			New Tool: cs-extract-key.py		 </h3>
	<div class="meta">Filed under:  <a href="https://blog.didierstevens.com/category/announcement/" rel="category tag">Announcement</a>,<a href="https://blog.didierstevens.com/category/encryption/" rel="category tag">Encryption</a>,<a href="https://blog.didierstevens.com/category/my-software/" rel="category tag">My Software</a> &#8212; Didier Stevens @ 0:00 <br /></div>

	<div class="storycontent">
		
<p>cs-extract-key.py is a tool designed to extract cryptographic keys from Cobalt Strike beacon process memory dumps.</p>
