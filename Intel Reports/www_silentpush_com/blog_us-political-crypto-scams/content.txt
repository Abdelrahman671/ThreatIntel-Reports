					<h1 class="entry-title">"Tis the season to be wary": Silent Push tracks Russia-linked threat actor involved in US political spoofing and crypto giveaway scams. Large cluster of IOFA domains discovered.</h1>                    <div class='ep-post-terms term-tag'><a class='ep-item-term ep-item-term-threat' href='https://www.silentpush.com/tag/threat/' >threat</a></div>					<div class="single-post-meta">

					</div>

												<div class="single-post-thumbnail "><img width="1920" height="1080" src="https://www.silentpush.com/wp-content/uploads/Untitled-design-15.png" class="wp-image-7564 avia-img-lazy-loading-7564 attachment-post-thumbnail size-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" alt="" decoding="async" loading="lazy" /></div>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="Executive-Summary">Executive Summary</h2>



<p>Silent Push Threat Analysts are tracking a threat actor, with links to Russia, who is actively deploying domains involved in crypto scams targeting the US Presidential Election, and prominent US tech brands.</p>



<p>The scams involve fake giveaways of Bitcoin and Ethereum cryptocurrency. Users are being asked to send coins to an attacker-controlled wallet, with the promise of double the amount of coins being returned to them.</p>



<p>Our research has unearthed a large number of websites featuring prominent US political figures, business leaders and global brand names advertising the fake giveaways, with counterfeit legal letters from US Government agencies attached to the domains to add legitimacy to the scam.</p>



<p>Individuals targeted include Former President Donald Trump, Vice President Kamala Harris, Apple CEO Tim Cook, Elon Musk, MicroStrategy co-founder Michael Saylor, Former Congressman Gene Green, Former Congressman Pete Olson, Peggy Kim (SEC), and Kerry O’Brien (FTC).</p>



<p>Brands and political institutions targeted include Apple, SpaceX, Donald Trump for President 2024 Campaign, MicroStrategy, Cardona Foundation, U.S. House of Representatives, U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, and the U.S. Federal Trade Commission.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="Background">Background</h2>



<p>Over the last 6 months, numerous reports have been published of websites using the image and likeness of <a href="https://qz.com/elon-musk-impersonation-scams-tesla-neuralink-spacex-1851298144">Elon Musk</a> and <a href="https://www.wired.com/story/maga-crypto-scammers-donald-trump/">Donald Trump</a> to propagate crypto scams, including <a href="https://www.techarp.com/crime/donald-trump-crypto-scam-alert/">AI deepfake videos</a>.</p>



<p>Threat actors have <a href="https://scammer.info/t/russian-crypto-scammers-hacking-big-youtubers-now-impersonating-former-president-trump/94280/55?page=3">hacked the YouTube channels</a> of prominent social media personalities, and international sporting events, before posting videos promoting the scam to millions of subscribers.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="Tracking-the-campaign">Tracking the campaign</h2>



<p>While investigating an unrelated campaign, our Threat Analysts discovered Indicator of Future Attack (IOFA) domains registered to the Russian email address <strong><a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection" class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="cca9a7fdf5f5fd8ca5a2b8a9bea2a9b8e2beb9">[email&#160;protected]</a></strong>, that suggested involvement in the aforementioned scams.</p>



<p>We executed an advanced content lookup using <strong><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rTu7V1spGWU&amp;t=10s&amp;ab_channel=SilentPush">Silent Push Web Scanner</a></strong>, and what we knew about the deployment, management and on-page content of the aforementioned infrastructure, that revealed a cluster of live scam domains with the following shared characteristics:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Registered to <strong><a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection" class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="45202e747c7c74052c2b3120372b20316b3730">[email&#160;protected]</a></strong></li>



<li>Hosted behind <strong>Cloudflare</strong></li>



<li>Similar themes, revolving around cryptocurrency, US financial/tech organizations,<strong> </strong>and the upcoming US Presidential Election<strong> </strong>(e.g. trumpdebate24[.]com), with <strong>matching body content</strong></li>



<li>Pages hidden behind a <strong>CAPTCHA</strong></li>



<li>Some pages featuring a <strong>chat function</strong></li>
</ul>



<p>One of the sites in the cluster features Russian language content &#8211; <strong>cryptologic[.]online</strong> &#8211; but seemingly isn’t involved in directly spoofing any organizations or individuals.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="Analyzing-malicious-domain-content">Analyzing domain content</h2>



<p>Although some domains in the cluster have been identified as malicious and taken down by Cloudflare, there are still a number of domains live as of writing.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="Mitigation">Mitigation</h2>



<p>Our Analysts are constructing a <a href="https://www.silentpush.com/blog/iofa-feed-analytics/">Silent Push IOFA Feed</a> containing all the scam domains gathered during our investigation.</p>
