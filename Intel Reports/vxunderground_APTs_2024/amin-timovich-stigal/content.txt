CAUTION 
Amin Timovich Stigal is wanted for his alleged involvement in criminal cyber activities between August of 2021 and February of 2022.  During that time, Stigal 
allegedly conspired to commit computer intrusions targeting Ukrainian critical infrastructure.  Stigal allegedly conspired to gain unauthorized access to computers 
associated with the Ukrainian government, resulting in the destruction of those systems in advance of the Russian invasion of Ukraine.  He further allegedly 
conspired to enable cyber activity against the United States government and private sector infrastructure.  The federal indictment charges Stigal with a computer 
hacking conspiracy intended to deploy destructive malware and take other disruptive actions.  A federal arrest warrant was issued for Stigal in the United States 
District Court, District of Maryland, Greenbelt, Maryland, on June 25, 2024, after he was charged with Conspiracy to Commit Computer Intrusion and Damage.
