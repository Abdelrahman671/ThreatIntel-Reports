  <h1 class="news">
      NCSC-NL cordially invites you to its research symposium Let’s do Cybersecurity Research Together</h1>
<p class="article-meta">
  News item | 20-09-2021 | 11:35</p><div class="intro"><p>At NCSC-NL we believe that better cybersecurity incident response requires more cooperation and research. Therefore, we proudly announce our upcoming research symposium <em>Let’s Do Cybersecurity Research Together</em>. This event is all about connecting multidisciplinary academic and applied cybersecurity research(ers) with professionals dealing with cybersecurity issues and incidents on a daily basis.</p></div>
  <p>We offer a varied program with keynotes from Dr. Jeroen van der Ham and Dr. Stephanie de Smale of NCSC-NL on <em>The Future of Cybersecurity Incident Response</em> and Dr. Corinne Cath of the University of Oxford on her PhD-research <em>Changing Minds and Machines</em>. The first day, TNO and Leiden University will also present outcomes of research conducted in collaboration with NCSC-NL. On day two we offer interesting round tables on <em>The Power of Internet Standards</em> and crypto communication and automated vulnerability research in dcypher’s community panel.</p>

<p>The symposium will take place digital from 1 to 5 pm (CEST) on both days. The full program and registration form can be found  here.</p></div>
