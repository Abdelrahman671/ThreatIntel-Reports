		<h1 itemprop="headline" class="post-title">Iranian Threat Agent Greenbug Impersonates Israeli High-Tech and Cyber Security Companies</h1>

                            <div class="entry-meta">
                    <span class="posted-on">Posted on <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2017-10-24T18:18:18+03:00">October 24, 2017</time></span><span class="byline"> by <span class="author vcard">ClearSky Research Team</span></span>                </div><!-- .entry-meta -->
            
		




	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p>Iranian Threat Agent <a href="https://www.clearskysec.com/ismagent/">Greenbug  </a>has been registering domains similar to those of Israeli High-Tech and Cyber Security Companies.</p>
<p>On 15 October 2017 a sample of <a href="https://researchcenter.paloaltonetworks.com/2017/07/unit42-oilrig-uses-ismdoor-variant-possibly-linked-greenbug-threat-group/">ISMdoor </a>was submitted to VirusTotal from Iraq.  The sample name was WmiPrv.tmp (f5ef3b060fb476253f9a7638f82940d9) and it had the following PDB string:</p>
<p><em>C:\Users\Void\Desktop\<strong>v 10.0.194</strong>\x64\Release\swchost.pdb</em></p>
<p>Two domains were used for command and control:</p>
<p><em>thetareysecurityupdate[.]com</em><br />
<em> securepackupdater[.]com</em></p>
<p><span id="more-1968"></span>By pivoting off the registration details and servers data of the two domains we discovered others registered by the threat agent. Eight contain the name of Israeli high-tech and cyber security companies and one of a Saudi Arabian testing &amp; commissioning of major electrical equipment company.</p>
<p>We estimate that the domains were registered in order to be used when targeting these companies, organisations related to them, or unrelated third parties. However, we do not have any indication that the companies were actually targeted or otherwise impacted.</p>
