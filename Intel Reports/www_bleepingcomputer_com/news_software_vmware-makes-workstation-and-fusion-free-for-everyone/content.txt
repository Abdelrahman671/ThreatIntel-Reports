<p style="text-align:center"><img alt="VMware" height="1080" src="https://www.bleepstatic.com/content/hl-images/2024/11/11/VMware.jpg" width="1920"></p>

<p>&#8203;VMware has announced that its VMware Fusion and VMware Workstation desktop hypervisors are now free to everyone for commercial, educational, and personal use.</p>

<p>In May, the company also made VMware Workstation Pro and Fusion Pro <a href="https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/software/vmware-makes-workstation-pro-and-fusion-pro-free-for-personal-use/" target="_blank">free for personal use</a>, allowing students and home users to set up virtualized test labs and experiment with other OSs by running virtual machines and Kubernetes clusters on Windows, Linux, and macOS devices.</p>

<p>Starting this week, the Pro versions and the two products will no longer be available under a paid subscription model.</p>

<p>"Effective immediately, both VMware Fusion and VMware Workstation will transition away from the paid subscription model, meaning you can now utilize these tools without any cost. The paid versions of these offerings &ndash; Workstation Pro and Fusion Pro &ndash; are no longer available for purchase," <a href="https://blogs.vmware.com/cloud-foundation/2024/11/11/vmware-fusion-and-workstation-are-now-free-for-all-users/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">said</a> Broadcom product marketing director Himanshu Singh.</p>

<p>"If you're currently under a commercial contract, you can rest easy knowing that your agreement will remain in effect until the end of your term. You will continue to receive the full level of service and enterprise-grade support as per your contract."</p>

<p>While the free versions will include all the features available in the paid products, Broadcom will no longer provide users with support ticketing for troubleshooting.</p>

<p>Broadcom plans to continue developing new features and improvements and ensure that updates are rolled out promptly.</p>

<p>"We're actively investing in new features, usability improvements, and other valuable enhancements," Singh added.&nbsp;"Our engineering teams are committed to maintaining our high standards for stability, with timely updates and reliable performance."</p>

<p>After&nbsp;signing into your Broadcom account, you can download VMware Fusion and VMware Workstation&nbsp;<a href="http://support.broadcom.com/group/ecx/downloads" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">on this page</a>.</p>

<p>This comes after Broadcom announced the <a href="https://blogs.vmware.com/cloud-foundation/2024/01/22/vmware-end-of-availability-of-perpetual-licensing-and-saas-services/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">end of perpetual licensing</a>, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20240507005726/https://knowledge.broadcom.com/external/article?legacyId=2107518" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">discontinuation of the free vSphere Hypervisor (ESXi)</a> offering, and the <a href="https://blogs.vmware.com/euc/2023/12/an-exciting-new-era-for-end-user-computing.html" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">divestiture of the End-User Computing Division</a> after acquiring VMware one year ago.</p>

function cz_strip_tags(input, allowed) {
  allowed = (((allowed || '') + '')
    .toLowerCase()
    .match(/<[a-z][a-z0-9]*>/g) || [])
    .join(''); // making sure the allowed arg is a string containing only tags in lowercase (<a><b><c>)
  var tags = /<\/?([a-z][a-z0-9]*)\b[^>]*>/gi,
    commentsAndPhpTags = /<!--[\s\S]*?-->|<\?(?:php)?[\s\S]*?\?>/gi;
  return input.replace(commentsAndPhpTags, '')
    .replace(tags, function($0, $1) {
      return allowed.indexOf('<' + $1.toLowerCase() + '>') > -1 ? $0 : '';
    });
}
function cz_br2nl(str) {
	var regex = /<br\s*[\/]?>/gi;
    //var pure_str = str.replace(regex,"\n");
    var pure_str = str.replace(regex,"");
    return cz_strip_tags(pure_str,'');
}
