<span face="&quot;trebuchet ms&quot; , sans-serif" style="background-color: white; color: #073763;">3. Decryption of your files with the help of third parties may cause increased price (they add their fee to&nbsp;</span><br />
<span face="&quot;trebuchet ms&quot; , sans-serif" style="background-color: white; color: #073763;">our) or you can become a victim of a scam.</span><br />
